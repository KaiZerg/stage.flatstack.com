SecurEdge Cloud is a platform that will be the point of management for each customer’s captive portal, billing, and Wi-Fi analytics. The main purpose is to provide managing SecurEdge Networks' customers wireless technologies in an easier and better way.
UI/UX, Front-end Development, RubyOnRails Development
 
 
EdgeManager Section
The clients will be able to see their buildings' coverage maps with the firewalls, controllers, location services and access points right on the floor plans to see how fast the connection is, how many clients they have in each building and how perfect the perfomance is.
 
 
Dashboard
Here the main features are the Usage Graph, which is showing the number of uploads and downloads on a timeline and miltiple pie charts with the Device types that end-users of the app are using and the most popular websites that are being visited.
 
 
Setting Page
The clients are also free to customize their captive portals, adding or removing social buttons, picking or uploading the background pics and logos and correcting pictures' transparency. Furthermore, there is a functionality of picking splash pages' working hours.
